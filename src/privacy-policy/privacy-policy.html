Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 16, 2025

CR Auto Detailing (“the Site”) uses an internal application named POSTAPI to display public Instagram posts related to our detailing work. This Privacy Policy explains what data we handle, how we use it, and how we protect your privacy.

What We Display

Our gallery showcases publicly available Instagram posts (images, captions, and links) from our official business Instagram account. These posts are fetched automatically through the Instagram Graph API to make it easier for clients to view our recent detailing work directly on the website.

Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal information from visitors browsing our website. The site functions as a portfolio and booking platform. If you use a contact form or booking system, that data is handled by our service provider and subject to their privacy policy.

Instagram API Usage

POSTAPI securely connects to Instagram using the official Instagram Graph API. The access token used to read and display posts belongs solely to CR Auto Detailing’s business Instagram account.

Access tokens are securely stored on our private server and are periodically refreshed to maintain functionality. These tokens do not allow access to personal data and are never exposed to website visitors.

Third-Party Services

Our website may contain embedded content or links from third parties (such as Instagram, Meta, or scheduling platforms). We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect our website and stored credentials from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. While no system is completely secure, we follow industry-standard practices to safeguard any technical data used by POSTAPI.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request content removal, please contact us at client@example.com.

This Privacy Policy is provided to satisfy Meta’s requirements for Instagram Graph API usage. POSTAPI is an internal integration used only by CR Auto Detailing to display public Instagram posts on the website.
